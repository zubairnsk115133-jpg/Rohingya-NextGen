About Ro NextGen

Who We Are

Ro NextGen (Rohingya NextGen) is an online learning center created to empower the next generation of Rohingya youth. Our platform provides educational resources, digital skills, and personal development opportunities to help learners achieve brighter futures.

Our Mission

To make education and digital knowledge accessible for every Rohingya learner, enabling them to grow with confidence and pursue new opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a strong and educated Rohingya generation equipped with modern skills, ready to lead positive change and contribute to the global community.

What We Offer

• Free and easy-to-access online courses
• Practical lessons in technology and digital skills
• Guidance for personal and career development
• A supportive community for Rohingya youth
